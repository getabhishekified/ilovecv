# iLoveCV — How to Use

**iLoveCV** tailors your resume to any job description using AI — entirely in your browser. Free, private, no sign-up needed.

---

## Before You Start

- Use **Chrome** or **Edge** (version 113 or newer) for the best experience — these support WebGPU, which powers the on-device AI
- Have your resume ready as a **PDF, DOCX, or TXT** file
- Have the **job description** copied to your clipboard

---

## Step 1 — Upload & Paste

1. Open [ilovecv.in](https://ilovecv.in) in your browser
2. Click the upload area (or drag and drop) to upload your resume
3. Paste the full job description into the text box on the right
4. Click **Analyze & Optimize**

> The AI model (~880 MB) will begin downloading in the background the first time. This takes 1–3 minutes depending on your connection. It's cached after that, so future visits are instant.

---

## Step 2 — Review the Analysis

While the AI loads, you'll see a breakdown of your resume vs. the job description:

- **Keyword Match Score** — how well your current resume matches the JD (0–100%)
- **Keywords** — green = already in your CV, red = missing (AI will add these)
- **Role & Tone** — the detected job type, seniority level, and writing style
- **Optimization Plan** — a preview of exactly what the AI will improve

When you're ready, click **Generate with AI**.

---

## Step 3 — Watch the AI Optimize in Real Time

The AI rewrites your resume live — you can watch it happen:

- The resume preview on the right updates section by section
- **Yellow highlights** = words that were changed or keywords that were added
- **Pink highlights** = placeholders where you should fill in your own numbers (e.g. "increased revenue by **[X]%**")
- A changelog on the left logs every improvement as it's made

The full optimization runs through 7 steps: headline, bullet points, skills, keyword injection, and a final polish pass.

---

## Step 4 — Download Your Optimized Resume

Once the AI is done:

1. Review the **Before / After** toggle to see the full diff
2. Fill in any **pink placeholders** with your real numbers
3. Click **Download PDF** or **Download DOCX** — your choice

> The PDF has smart page breaks so no text is ever cut off. The DOCX is a fully editable Word document.

---

## Tips for Best Results

- **Paste the full JD** — the more context the AI has, the better the keyword matching
- **Use a clean resume** — if your CV is messy or image-based, extract the text first and save it as a TXT file
- **Fill in the pink placeholders** — the AI leaves `[X]` gaps intentionally so your final numbers are accurate
- **Re-run for each application** — one tailored resume per job beats one generic resume for all jobs

---

## Privacy

Everything runs locally in your browser. Your resume and the job description are never sent to any server. The AI model runs on your own device using WebGPU.
